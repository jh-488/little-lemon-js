About Us

Who we are ?

Welcome to Little Lemon, a warm and inviting Mediterranean restaurant that has been a labor of love for our family for generations. Nestled in the heart of Chicago, we take immense pride in bringing the rich flavors of the Mediterranean to your plate, with a contemporary twist that makes dining with us a truly unique and unforgettable experience.

Traditional Recipes, Modern Twist

We believe in honoring the authenticity of Mediterranean cuisine, while also infusing it with a modern twist that excites the palate.

Local and Sustainable Ingredients

We take our commitment to quality seriously. That's why we source our ingredients locally whenever possible, supporting our community.

Warm Hospitality and Inviting Atmosphere

At Little Lemon, we treat our guests like an extension of our own family. We are dedicated to providing exceptional service for your dining experience.

A Culinary Journey and Gastronomic Adventure

Step inside Little Lemon and embark on a culinary journey that takes you through the vibrant streets of the Mediterranean.
